Below is the 12-Feature MVP that can realistically sell.

Think of it as three layers of value:

Website generation

Business features

Deployment & usability

If these 12 exist, someone can generate, publish, and get leads from a site.

Smooth Builder MVP (12 Features That Sell)

The first step in the product.

User selects:

City
Company name
Services
Logo
Contact details

Home

Contact

A killer feature for businesses.

/accounting-bern
